The Central Problem: Declining Vision and Eye Health

Is Your Vision Deteriorating Daily?

The primary requirement for normal vision is the elasticity of the crystalline lens or the eye lens. Unfortunately, this ability diminishes under the influence of various factors:

  • Age
  • Excessive eye strain
  • Various pathological processes (inflammation)
  • Insufficient blood supply
  • Myasthenia gravis and muscle weakness

The main reason for vision loss is often linked to the **dysfunction of eye muscles**!

The muscular system of the eye focuses images onto the retina. Disruptions in the eye muscles lead to pressure imbalances within the eyeball, causing the lens to become distorted or damaged. This results in blurred vision.

Contact lenses and glasses are not a permanent solution and can worsen eye damage by relaxing eye muscles, leading to further problems.
