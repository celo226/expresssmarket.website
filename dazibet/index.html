Understanding the Severe Risks of Type 2 Diabetes

Diabetes symptoms can appear at any moment and lead to a cascade of serious health issues:

  • Chronic Kidney Failure: Gradual and continuous damage to kidney tissue.
  • Blindness: Irreversible loss of vision.
  • Necrosis: Tissue death, potentially leading to limb loss.
  • Nerve Damage (Neuropathy): Abnormal nervous system function, affecting blood vessel function.
  • Heart Attack: Insufficient oxygen-rich blood supply to the heart.
  • Cerebral Vascular Disease (Stroke): Lack of blood and oxygen to the brain due to ruptured or blocked blood vessels, causing brain cell death.
  • Vascular Disease: Vascular defects that can lead to severe health consequences.
