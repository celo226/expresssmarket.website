Understanding Where Fat Comes From

Abdomen Fat

Excessive intake of sugary and fatty foods without nutrient-rich options, often seen in office workers who snack heavily but neglect exercise.

Face Fat

Accumulation of fat on the face due to consuming too much salty or high-salt foods, leading to water retention and puffiness.

Thigh Fat

Losing fat from the thighs can enhance the appearance of the hips, creating a more balanced and proportionate silhouette.

Arm Fat

Fat buildup in the arms due to excessive weight gain, causing sagging and a less attractive appearance.

Root Causes of Excess Fat

Genetic Predisposition

Your genes can influence fat storage and distribution, often linked with shared lifestyle habits within families.

Lifestyle Habits

Smoking, insufficient sleep, unbalanced diets, and excessive alcohol consumption disrupt physical balance.

Hormonal Imbalance

Hormonal fluctuations can lead to fat accumulation regardless of your individual profile.

Environmental Factors

Overeating, demanding work schedules, and a lack of healthy food options contribute to weight gain.
